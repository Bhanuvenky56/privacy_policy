Privacy Policy - AI Story Generator

Last updated: March 2025

Welcome to AI Story Generator. This app allows users to upload images and generate AI-powered stories based on those images. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Uploaded Images

  • Images you upload for story generation
  • Image metadata (if available)

1.2 Generated Content

  • AI-generated stories
  • Story titles and prompts

1.3 Usage Data

  • App interaction data
  • Device type and OS version
  • Log and error reports

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To analyze images and generate stories
  • To improve AI performance
  • To enhance user experience
  • To fix bugs and technical issues

3. Image & Data Storage

Uploaded images are processed only to generate stories. We do not permanently store images unless required for app functionality. Images may be automatically deleted after processing.

4. Data Security

We use secure servers and encrypted communication to protect your data. We do not sell, trade, or misuse your data.

5. Third-Party Services

  • AI service providers for story generation
  • Cloud storage providers (if used)

These services follow industry-standard privacy and security practices.

6. Children's Privacy

AI Story Generator is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. User Rights

  • Stop using the app anytime
  • Request data deletion
  • Control uploaded content

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Updates will appear on this page.
